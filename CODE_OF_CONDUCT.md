# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project is a technical specification effort. We want participation to be
productive, respectful, and focused on the work. Everyone interacting in this
repository's issues, pull requests, and discussions is expected to uphold this
standard.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional. Critique ideas and text, not people.
- Argue from the technical merits: interoperability, security, clarity, and
  fidelity to the specification.
- Assume good faith, and give others the benefit of the doubt.
- Accept that normative decisions are made deliberately and may not go your way.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or demeaning comments.
- Discriminatory or derogatory language of any kind.
- Sustained disruption of discussion, or deliberate bad-faith argument.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests,
commit discussions) and when an individual is representing the project in public
spaces.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported privately to:

    npamp-editor@bubblefish.sh

Reports will be reviewed and handled as confidentially as practical. The
maintainer may take any action deemed appropriate, including warnings, removal
of comments or contributions, or banning a participant from the repository.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open-source community standards and
is maintained by the project maintainer.
